In the Name of Almighty God. 
This is a Treaty of Peace & Friendship, established between Us and the United States of America.
Article 1: We declare that both Parties have agreed that this Treaty consisting of twenty five Articles shall be inserted in this Book and delivered to the Honorable Thomas Barclay, the Agent of the United States...
Article 10: ...the Citizens of America our good Friends.
